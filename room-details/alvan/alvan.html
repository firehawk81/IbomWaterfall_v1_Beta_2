Spacious Elegance

Named after a renowned Son of Aro and foremost Nigerian Educationist, Alvan Ikoku suite features an expansive living room that provides the perfect atmosphere for intimate gatherings, private parties or quiet moments of reflection. Natural light cascades through large windows, enhancing the suite's refined character and creating a warm and captivating ambiance.

Refined Retreats

The suite features 4 exquisite bedrooms, each offering a King-sized bed that promises restful nights and rejuvenating mornings. These serene sanctuaries are designed with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring an atmosphere of intimacy and relaxation. Soft, luxurious linens envelop you, while thoughtfully curated decor infuses each room with a sense of style and sophistication. Every moment spent in these tranquil retreats allows you to luxuriate in the comfort and exclusivity that define the Ibom Waterfall Resort & Suites experience.

Unparalleled Indulgence

Beyond the aesthetic allure, the Alvan Ikoku suite is equipped with premier amenities that cater to your every desire. The suite provides a private dining area with integrated kitchenette refrigerator and microwave – which makes it a popular choice for guests with family and young children.

The exceptional butler service stands ready to anticipate your needs, ensuring a seamless and indulgent experience throughout your stay. Stay connected with free high speed wireless internet access, entertain yourself via a selection of digital content options on the large screen smart tv and savor the wide selection of drinks available at the minibar. Every detail is crafted to enhance your stay, making it not just a destination but a personal sanctuary of unparalleled indulgence.
